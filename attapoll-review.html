What Is AttaPoll?

AttaPoll is a mobile-first survey app that connects users with market research studies. Companies pay to get consumer opinions on their products and services — AttaPoll distributes those surveys to users and splits the payment. You answer surveys, they pay you. Simple.

Founded in 2016 and based in the UK, AttaPoll has grown into one of the most widely used survey apps globally. It has a 4.3-star rating on the App Store with tens of thousands of reviews and operates in over 30 countries. Unlike many survey sites that feel like they were built in 2008, AttaPoll has a clean, modern interface and a transparent payout system.

What separates AttaPoll from most competitors is the referral program — you earn a percentage of what the people you refer earn from surveys, permanently. That's the piece that makes this genuinely interesting as a side hustle rather than just spare change.
