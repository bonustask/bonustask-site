🔍 How Survey Matching Works

Platforms match you to surveys based on your demographic profile — age, location, income, household, employment, and more. The more complete and accurate your profile, the more surveys you're eligible for, and the fewer screener disqualifications you'll hit.

Think of your profile as your resume. A complete resume gets more interviews. An empty one gets skipped. Fill it out on every platform before you take your first survey.

  • Employment status — full-time, part-time, and self-employed all unlock different survey pools
  • Household income bracket — honest answers unlock financial and consumer product surveys
  • Homeowner vs renter — real estate and home improvement surveys pay above average
  • Household size + children's ages — parenting surveys are premium-priced and frequent
  • Vehicle ownership + make/model — automotive surveys are short and pay well
  • Health conditions (where asked) — pharma surveys are longer but pay $3–$10 each
  • Job industry and title — B2B surveys targeting professionals pay $5–$20 per survey
  • Brand usage and shopping habits — CPG surveys are the most frequent category overall

🎮 How Game Offers Work

Game offers are performance-based. You download a game, reach a specific milestone (level 10, Chapter 5, first in-app structure built), and get paid the listed amount when you hit it. The payout is fixed and shown upfront — no guessing.

Strategy tips to maximize your time:

  • Always read the full requirement — "Reach level 10" vs "Reach level 10 within 7 days" are completely different challenges
  • Check if the milestone is free to reach — if progression is paywalled, skip the offer entirely
  • Pick high payout offers over fast ones — a $10 offer that takes 4 hours beats a $1 offer that takes 30 minutes
  • Run one game offer at a time — splitting focus slows progress and risks missing deadlines
  • Screenshot your progress at key milestones — if a reward fails to track, screenshots are your evidence for a dispute
  • Stack offers sequentially — finish one game, immediately pick the next highest payout available

🏆 Game Types That Pay Best

Best ROI
  • Strategy / city builders — Rise of Kingdoms, Evony — high milestone payouts
  • RPG / level-based games — clear progression = predictable tracking
  • Puzzle games — fast to level, easy milestone completion
  • Casino / slot apps — fast progression, no real money required
  • Idle / clicker games — run in background, low active effort
Avoid These
  • Pay-to-win games — free progression too slow to hit milestones
  • Sports manager games — locked behind real-time timers
  • Vague milestone requirements — "be active 7 days" often doesn't track
  • Competitive / battle royale games — skill-dependent, progress not guaranteed
