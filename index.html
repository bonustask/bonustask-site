🎯 Build a Dream Survey Profile

Survey platforms match you to surveys by demographics. A complete, accurate profile means more surveys, better pay, and fewer disqualifications. Fill these out on every platform you use:

  • Employment status — full-time, part-time, and self-employed all unlock different survey pools
  • Household income bracket — unlocks financial and consumer product surveys
  • Homeowner vs renter — real estate and home improvement surveys pay well and are frequent
  • Household size + children's ages — parenting surveys are premium-priced and high volume
  • Vehicle ownership + make/model — automotive surveys are short and pay above average
  • Health conditions (where asked) — pharma surveys are longer but pay $3–$10 each
  • Job industry and title — B2B surveys targeting professionals pay $5–$20 per survey
  • Brand usage and shopping habits — CPG surveys (food, cleaning, personal care) are the most frequent

✅ Which Survey Types Pay Best

Take These
  • Pharmaceutical / health — 10–25 min, pay $3–$10
  • Automotive ownership — fast, $1–$4 each
  • B2B / professional — rare but $5–$20 per survey
  • Political / public opinion — fast, frequent, good volume
  • New product concepts — 5–12 min, solid payout ratio
  • Parenting / baby products — high demand, pays above average
  • Finance and investing — if you qualify, these pay well
Skip These
  • Sweepstakes-only surveys — no guaranteed payout ever
  • 30+ minute surveys under $1 — bad time-to-pay ratio
  • 10+ screener questions — high disqualification rate
  • Academic research with no pay — sometimes disguised as surveys
  • Webcam-required surveys — unless pay is confirmed upfront
  • Diary studies with daily tasks — commitment risk for uncertain payout

🎮 How Game Offers Work

Game offers are performance-based. You download a game, play to a specific milestone (reach level 10, build X structure, make first purchase), and get paid when you hit it. The payout is set before you start — no surprises.

  • Read the requirement in full — "Reach level 10" vs "Reach level 10 within 7 days" are completely different challenges
  • Check if it's playable free to the milestone — if progression is paywalled, skip it
  • Higher milestones = higher pay per hour — a $8 offer to reach Chapter 5 often beats a $1 offer for 20 minutes of gameplay
  • Run one game offer at a time — splitting focus slows progress and risks missing a deadline
  • Screenshot your progress at key points — if a reward doesn't track, screenshots are your proof when disputing

🏆 Game Types That Pay Best

Best ROI
  • Strategy / city builders — Rise of Kingdoms, Evony — high milestone payouts
  • RPG / level-based games — clear progression = predictable milestone tracking
  • Puzzle games — fast to level, easy to hit requirements
  • Casino / slot apps — fast progression, decent pay, no real money required
  • Idle / clicker games — run in background, easy milestone completion
Skip These
  • Pay-to-win games — free progression is too slow without spending
  • Sports manager games — progression locked behind real-time timers
  • Games with vague milestones — "be active for 7 days" often doesn't track properly
  • Battle royale / competitive games — skill-dependent, progress not guaranteed

📱 App Download & Free Trial Offers

Beyond games, both platforms have app install and trial offers — some of the fastest earners available.
