Step 2 — Build Traffic on Each Platform

📘 Facebook Groups

Fastest free traffic. People in "make money from home" groups are actively looking for what you have. The key is warm-up before posting.

  • Join 15–25 groups — "make money from home", "side hustle [your city]", "work from home [province/state]"
  • Comment and help for 2 weeks before posting anything — establishes you as a real person, not a spammer
  • Post earnings screenshots, never straight promo — "Made $47 this week on my phone, here's what I use" converts 5x better than "join this app"
  • Put your link in the comments, not the post body — Facebook algorithmically suppresses posts with external links
  • Native video outperforms text by 3–5x — a 60-second screen recording of your earnings balance

💬 Reddit — Evergreen Traffic

Reddit answers rank on Google and keep sending traffic for years. This is your slow-burn channel that pays forever once established.

  • Target subreddits — r/beermoney, r/sidehustle, r/WorkOnline, r/povertyfinance
  • Answer questions honestly and thoroughly — 300–500 words, include real pros AND cons. Reddit users are cynical and reward honesty.
  • Disclose your referral link explicitly — "This is my referral link — full disclosure." Honesty converts better than hiding it and avoids bans.
  • Target questions like — "Is AttaPoll legit?", "Best survey apps?", "How to make extra money on my phone?"
  • Post 2–3 times per week for the first month — volume builds your account karma which boosts visibility

🌐 Quora — Google SEO Traffic

Quora answers frequently rank in Google's top 10. Write one good answer today and it can send you referrals for the next 3 years.

  • Search Quora for — "Is AttaPoll legit?", "Best survey apps Canada", "Apps that pay real money to PayPal"
  • Write 400–600 word answers — Quora rewards detailed answers with more visibility
  • Set up a strong bio — "Side income researcher | I test apps that pay" — gives you authority
  • 2–3 answers per day for the first month — then Google starts ranking your answers within 3–6 months

Common Mistakes That Kill Referral Growth

  • Posting your link before you have earnings to show — nobody trusts a referral from someone who has not used the product. Earn first.
  • Only using one platform — Facebook alone will not scale to $1,000/month. Stack TikTok + Reddit + Quora for compounding reach.
  • Optimizing for signups instead of activations — ghost accounts pay you nothing. Tell every new referral to complete their profile immediately.
  • Not tracking which platform converts — without Bitly data you cannot double down on what works. Set this up from day one.
  • Quitting in month 1 — referral income builds slowly and then compounds fast. The people who give up at week 3 miss the inflection point.
