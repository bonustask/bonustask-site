How Much Can Canadians Actually Earn?

Canada is a Tier A market — good earnings, not the best. Here's what's realistic:

  • Casual use (10 min/day): $30–$75/month across AttaPoll + FreeCash
  • Consistent use (20–30 min/day): $150–$450/month
  • Active use (45+ min/day): $400–$700/month
  • With referral income (100 active referrals): Add $150–$300/month passively

The biggest variable isn't effort — it's your demographic profile. A Canadian homeowner who owns a vehicle, works full-time, and has children will qualify for significantly more surveys than someone who leaves their profile incomplete. Fill out your profile 100% on every platform before taking your first survey.
